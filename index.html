Game Overview

Welcome to Happy Home, where every merge is a step closer to building the cozy sanctuary of your dreams.
You join Ava, a young designer returning to her family’s old cottage after years away. Dusty rooms, forgotten blueprints, hidden treasures await — all waiting for your creative touch.


Uncover sentimental mementos, unlock themed rooms, and merge your way through the renovation of a lifetime. Whether it’s a sunlit reading nook or a charming garden patio, your design choices shape the entire home.

Design. Restore. Remember. This isn’t just decoration — it’s healing through creativity.

Gameplay Features

  • Merge to Design: Match decor pieces, furniture, and tools to unlock advanced items and surprises.

  • Room by Room Restoration: Renovate cozy spaces from the attic to the garden, each with unique moods and goals.

  • Story-Rich Progression: Reveal family secrets, childhood memories, and Ava’s emotional journey along the way.

  • Custom Styles: Choose between rustic, boho, minimalist, or eclectic decor themes — your taste, your call.

  • Offline Play: Your happy home is always there, even without internet.

  • Relaxing Vibes: No timers, no stress — just peaceful play with soft music and ambient details.

  • Ad-Free Bliss: Enjoy uninterrupted decorating time with zero ads.

  • Eco-Inspired Designs: Discover themes centered on sustainability, nature, and upcycled charm.

How to Play

  1. Arrive at Home: Ava steps into the dusty foyer of her late grandmother’s house. It’s full of memories and mysteries.

  2. Merge Objects: Combine tools, fabrics, furniture parts, and decorations to create stunning interior items.

  3. Design & Choose: Unlock room themes and decide your own layout, colors, and style.

  4. Discover the Story: From old letters to vintage photo albums, every merge uncovers a piece of family history.

  5. Complete the House: Finish rooms, win hearts, and maybe discover Ava’s next chapter.
