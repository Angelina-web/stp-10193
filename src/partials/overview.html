Game Overview

Welcome to Happy Home, where every merge is a step closer to building the cozy sanctuary of your dreams.
You join Ava, a young designer returning to her family’s old cottage after years away. Dusty rooms, forgotten blueprints, and hidden treasures await — all waiting for your creative touch.


Uncover sentimental mementos, unlock themed rooms, and merge your way through the renovation of a lifetime. Whether it’s a sunlit reading nook or a charming garden patio, your design choices shape the entire home.

Design. Restore. Remember. This isn’t just decoration — it’s healing through creativity.
